Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

For Salon Customers

Q
How do I book an appointment?
A
You can easily book an appointment by visiting our website, selecting your preferred salon, and choosing an available time slot.
Q
What are happy hour prices?
A
Happy hour prices are special discounted rates offered during specific times. Check the salon’s profile for details on when these discounts apply.
Q
Can I change or cancel my appointment?
A
Yes! You can modify or cancel your appointment directly through your account on our website. Just make sure to do it within the salon’s cancellation policy timeframe.
Q
Is my payment secure?
A
Absolutely! We use industry-standard encryption to ensure that all your payment information is secure.
Q
What if the salon I want isn’t listed?
A
We’re constantly expanding our network. If your favourite salon isn’t listed, let us know, and we’ll do our best to reach out to them!

For Salon Owners

Q
How do I sign up my salon?
A
Simply visit our website and fill out the registration form. Our team will get in touch to guide you through the setup process.
Q
How can I manage my salon's availability?
A
Our platform allows you to easily update your calendar in real-time, so clients can see available slots instantly.
Q
What kind of insights will I receive?
A
You’ll receive detailed reports on revenue, client bookings, and suggestions for implementing happy hour pricing to boost profitability.
Q
Are there fees for using your platform?
A
Yes, there may be a small subscription fee or a commission based on bookings. Please check our pricing page for more details.
Q
Can I customize my salon’s profile?
A
Absolutely! You can personalize your salon’s profile with images, descriptions, services offered, and pricing to attract more clients.
